Privacy Policy

App: Glam Pro

Bundle ID: com.liuying.glampro

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Glam Pro ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Glam Pro iOS application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account and session data: anonymous account identifiers, authentication/session tokens, and related status data needed to keep you signed in and deliver app features.
  • Device and app data: basic device/app metadata (for example, app version and technical diagnostics) and app event data used for analytics and attribution.
  • Purchase data: subscription and consumable purchase information needed to verify purchases and update subscription/credit status.
  • User content: photos/videos you choose to upload for AI generation and generated outputs returned to you.
  • Feedback data: information you submit through in-app feedback/contact channels.

2. Permissions We Use

  • Camera: to capture content when you choose to use camera-based features.
  • Photo Library (Read): to let you select existing media.
  • Photo Library (Add): to save generated media to your device.
  • Tracking (ATT): permission is requested under Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework for attribution/measurement where applicable.

You can manage permissions in iOS Settings at any time. If you deny a permission, related features may not work fully.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide core app functions, including media upload, AI task creation, and result delivery.
  • Authenticate users, maintain sessions, and protect service security.
  • Process and verify subscriptions and credits through Apple's in-app purchase systems.
  • Measure app performance, installation/purchase attribution, and improve product quality.
  • Respond to support or feedback requests.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate the App, including:

  • Apple (StoreKit): in-app purchase processing and receipt-related verification workflows.
  • Meta/Facebook SDK: analytics and attribution-related app events.
  • Backend infrastructure providers (e.g., Supabase-hosted APIs): account/session handling, task processing, and content-related service operations.

These providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

5. Tracking and ATT

Where required, tracking-related data access depends on your ATT choice. If you decline tracking permission, attribution capabilities may be limited, but core app functionality remains available.

6. Data Storage and Retention

  • Authentication tokens may be stored securely on device (for example, in iOS Keychain).
  • Certain app state/preferences may be stored locally (for example, in UserDefaults).
  • Uploaded and generated media may be processed/stored on backend services as needed to provide features.

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

7. Your Choices and Rights

  • Control permissions through iOS Settings.
  • Request support through the in-app contact/feedback entry.
  • You may uninstall the App at any time to stop further local data use.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by local law), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted with a revised "Effective Date".

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the in-app feedback/contact page.
